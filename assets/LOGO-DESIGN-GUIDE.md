## Design Rationale

### Why This Design Works

1. **Memorable**: Simple, distinctive shape
2. **Scalable**: Clear at all sizes (16px to 1024px)
3. **Meaningful**: Deep cultural and service connection
4. **Versatile**: Works in color and monochrome
5. **Timeless**: Based on natural forms and classic principles
6. **Professional**: Follows 2026 minimalist trends
7. **Warm**: Humanized minimalism, not sterile

### Compared to Original Logo

**Original**:
- Abstract geometric shapes
- Unclear meaning
- Difficult to recognize

**New "Blooming Connection"**:
- Clear metaphor (two people connecting)
- Culturally meaningful (sakura)
- Service-relevant (personal guide)
- Instantly recognizable

- Color accuracy: Use exact hex codes
- Background contrast: Ensure visibility
