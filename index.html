Meet Your Guide

Hi, I'm Sammy!

Japanese, raised in Osaka for 20 years, now working in Tokyo. I've studied and interned in Sweden, worked full-time in Indonesia, and spent 5 years living with foreigners in shared houses.

I've shared daily life with 500+ international residents in Japan. Working in marketing in a global environment, I understand both the Japanese perspective and the challenges foreigners face.

Through my Instagram (@sakura.talk), I share hidden gems, honest thoughts, and daily life in Japan with 13K+ followers. Now I want to connect with you personally through 1-on-1 sessions.
